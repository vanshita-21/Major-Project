Frequently Asked Questions

What is an influencer?

An influencer is a person who has a following on social media and can influence their followers' purchasing decisions.

How does your platform work?

Our platform connects brands with influencers. Brands can search for influencers based on specific criteria and collaborate with them to promote their products or services.

How do I become an influencer on your platform?

To become an influencer on our platform, you must have a following on social media and create a profile. Brands will be able to find you based on your profile and you can start collaborating with them.

What are the fees for using your platform?

There are no fees for influencers to use our platform. Brands will be charged a fee for using our services.

What happens if there is a dispute between a brand and an influencer?

We have a dispute resolution process in place. Both the brand and the influencer will be able to present their case and we will make a decision based on the evidence presented.
